Local Artisans 

 

These are some of the artisans who sell their products near the beach or at events such as the Martina fair, which takes place every Friday from 4:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. in Matapalo. 

 

Eliza 

 

Artisan with 18 years of experience crafting kitchen utensils from different types of wood, finished with vegetable oil. Prices start at $10. 

 

Elmer 

 

Painter and writer with 36 years of experience. His painting style is realism and imaginative figurative art. As a writer, he is the author of 5 published books, including Bajo el agrio sol and Radio Soledad. Painting prices start at $25. 

 

Claudia 

 

Specialist in encapsulating real butterfly wings, provided by an authorized butterfly farm. Prices range from $8 to $25. 

 

Hubert 

 

Creates pendants, earrings, and bracelets representing the flora and fauna of Corcovado, using wood and coconut shells.

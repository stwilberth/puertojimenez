# Aventuras Café

Opened in 2021 in the Pueblo Viejo neighbourhood near Playa Las Gaviotas, Aventuras Café quickly found its audience: travellers who want something beyond rice and beans, and locals who've been waiting for exactly this. The menu covers breakfast, lunch, and dinner with a focus on fresh, creative cooking.

The coffee is properly roasted and the kombucha is made on-site. The coconut yuca cake alone is worth the detour.

## Highlights

- **Fresh-roasted coffee** — espresso and filter, done with care
- **Coconut yuca cake** — a signature not to be missed
- **Tuna burger & chicken fajitas** — crowd favourites
- **Organic juices & kombucha** — made in-house
- **Location** — Pueblo Viejo, near Playa Las Gaviotas

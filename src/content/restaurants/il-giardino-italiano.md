# Il Giardino Italiano

A proper Italian bistro in the middle of the jungle — and it delivers. The pizza dough is hand-stretched, the pasta is made in-house, and the ingredients are sourced with care. The signature **Pizza Loreto** — topped with avocado and onion paste instead of tomato — has become something of a local legend.

The garden setting feels relaxed and unhurried, making it a natural choice for a slower dinner after a long day on the trails.

## Highlights

- **Pizza Loreto** — avocado and onion paste base, a must-try
- **Al pesto pasta** — rich, fragrant, and generously portioned
- **Garden atmosphere** — open-air seating under the canopy
- **Value** — high quality at fair prices by any standard

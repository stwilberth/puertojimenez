# Marisquería Corcovado

A waterfront staple in Puerto Jiménez with consistently high ratings and a loyal following among locals and visitors alike. The menu centers on fresh-caught seafood — ceviche, grilled fish fillets, garlic shrimp, and bocas (appetizers) — served in generous portions at honest prices.

The breezy beachfront setting makes it a go-to spot for lunch or sunset drinks after a day in Corcovado or on the water.

## Highlights

- **Ceviche** — bright, citrusy, and made fresh daily
- **Grilled whole fish** — seasonal catch prepared simply and well
- **Bocas** — great for sharing over cold drinks
- **Location** — right on the waterfront, ideal for people-watching

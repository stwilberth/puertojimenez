# Pizzería Carolina

Run by a Sicilian owner-chef who takes the craft seriously. The dough, the sauce, the toppings — everything is made with the kind of attention that earns a 4.7 rating across 176 reviews. This isn't a novelty restaurant riding on its jungle location; the food stands on its own.

Located in the town centre, it's easy to find and consistently delivers. Expect a warm, unhurried welcome and a menu that goes well beyond margherita.

## Highlights

- **Handmade pizzas** — Sicilian technique, tropical setting
- **Pasta dishes** — rotating selection, always fresh
- **Family atmosphere** — small, personal, never rushed
- **Rating** — 4.7 / 5 on TripAdvisor (176 reviews)

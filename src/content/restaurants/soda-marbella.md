# Soda Marbella

One of those humble spots that locals know and visitors discover by word of mouth. Soda Marbella punches well above its price point — the ceviche is sharp and fresh, the garlic fish is cooked with care, and the cream sauce with mushrooms has earned a dedicated following.

Portions are generous, prices are some of the lowest in town, and the cooking has the kind of straightforward confidence that only comes from years of doing it right.

## Highlights

- **Ceviche** — a local favourite, made to order
- **Garlic fish** — simple, fragrant, and satisfying
- **Casado** — the classic Costa Rican plate done well

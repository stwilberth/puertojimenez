# Elmer Rodríguez Campos

Elmer is a seasoned artist with over 36 years of experience in the world of painting. Since 1990, he has been dedicated to creating murals that not only beautify spaces but also tell stories and foster community engagement.

## Artistic Journey

He has painted murals throughout Costa Rica, leaving a colorful legacy in numerous communities. Beyond his personal artistic expression, Elmer has led significant projects focused on the rehabilitation of public spaces in collaboration with social groups at risk.

## Social Impact

A key part of Elmer's mission is using art as a tool for social inclusion. He has worked extensively with:
- Youth in addiction recovery programs.
- Individuals in situations of abandonment.
- Various social institutions where he has taught art courses.

His work demonstrates that art can be a powerful catalyst for healing and social change.
